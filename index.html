▶ How To Play

  • Roll the dice, the outcome will be added to the Current.
  • After that you can either hold or roll again.
  • If you hold the current then it will be added to your Score.
  • If the outcome is 1 then the current will be restored to 0 and your turn will be passed.
  • The Player to reach 100 score first will WIN!
